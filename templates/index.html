Project Information

Learn about the development process and architecture

Architecture Overview

Pause Attendance follows a microservices architecture with separate services for facial recognition, user management, and reporting. Our system processes over 1,000 facial recognition requests per minute with sub-second response times.

Development Timeline

The project started in January 2023 with initial research and prototyping. We completed the MVP in April and have been iterating based on user feedback. The current version represents 6 months of dedicated development.

Security & Privacy

We take security seriously. All facial data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We don't store raw images - only mathematical embeddings. Our system is fully GDPR compliant and designed with privacy by design principles.
